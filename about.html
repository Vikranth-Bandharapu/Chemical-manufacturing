Who We Are

Driving Global Chemical Manufacturing

We are a premier global chemical company, dedicated to solving complex industrial challenges through innovative chemistry.

Since our founding, we have pushed the boundaries of what is possible, synthesizing thousands of distinct molecules that form the building blocks of pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and modern infrastructure.

Our Mission

To manufacture high-quality, sustainable chemical products that enable our partners across the globe to build a safer, healthier, and more advanced world.

Our Vision

To be the universally recognized leader in chemical innovation, setting the benchmark for zero-emission manufacturing and molecular precision.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Innovation

1996

Chemco was founded with a single small-scale reactor in New York.

2005

Expanded into European markets and opened our first international R&D center.

2018

Achieved 100% carbon-neutral operations across all North American facilities.

2026

Launching our largest state-of-the-art green chemistry complex globally.

Infrastructure

Global Manufacturing Network

Our footprint spans 12 advanced manufacturing plants across 4 continents. We operate multi-purpose synthesis plants, specialized polymer units, and high-containment API facilities.

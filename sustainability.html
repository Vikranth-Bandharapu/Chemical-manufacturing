Our Promise

Chemistry for a Greener Tomorrow

We integrate Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into every aspect of our operations. From reducing emissions to supporting local communities, sustainability is the foundation of our long-term strategy.

Targeting Net-Zero

We have pledged to achieve Net-Zero greenhouse gas emissions across all global operations by 2040. We are actively transitioning our energy mix to renewables and optimizing our supply chain logistics.

45%

Reduction since 2015

100%

Renewable energy goal

Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD)

Water is a critical resource. Our flagship manufacturing plants operate on a closed-loop Zero Liquid Discharge system, ensuring that no industrial wastewater is released into the environment. Every drop is treated, purified, and reused in our cooling towers and boilers.

15 Million Liters

Water saved annually

Circular Economy & Waste

Solvent Recovery

Over 85% of process solvents are distilled and recovered for reuse within our plants.

Eco-Packaging

Transitioning to biodegradable and 100% recyclable containers for all commercial shipments.

Byproduct Utilization

Partnering with construction firms to utilize chemical byproducts as cement additives.

Principles of Green Chemistry

Our R&D focuses on designing products and processes that minimize the use and generation of hazardous substances. We emphasize atom economy, less hazardous chemical syntheses, and the use of renewable feedstocks.

Occupational Health & Safety

The safety of our workforce is paramount. We maintain a "Goal Zero" policy for workplace incidents, enforced through rigorous training, automated hazardous material handling, and state-of-the-art PPE protocols.

ISO 45001 Certified

Corporate Social Responsibility

STEM Education

Community Health
